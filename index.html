SimCity 201

An agent-oriented simulations of citizens in a city. The people of SimCity 201 have jobs (or not), travel around town, have physiological and psychological needs, and commit crimes. Assign them actions, set them on a new career path, or just watch them go about their their lives.

Developed in Java with basic animation using Swing. Shortlog

A personal digital micro-journal primarily interfaced via your phone. Users text entries to the Shortlog number, which stores the logs in your private journal. Web log-in, which requires phone authentication rather than a username or password, allows viewing past entries, entry patterns, and mood/entry analysis.

Styled the front end of the web portal with CSS and Javascript. Created in a team of 5 at the 2013 LA Hacks, placing in the top 5 out of 44 teams.

Audio Alert

Audio Alert, or AAlert, is an Android-based phone app that analyzes sounds from the environment for emergency alerts, such as fire alarms and police sirens, and notifies its hearing-impaired users.

Implemented vibrational and toast notification system, and designed the app's graphics and interface. Created in a team of 3 for the 2013 Project: Possibility SS12 Hackathon where it won 1st Place. Later won 1st place in the USC Transmedia Forum in mobile.
